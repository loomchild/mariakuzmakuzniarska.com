I am a Paris-based artist, specializing in artistic nudes. My work focuses on the form and the motion of the human figure and is deeply grounded in my biomedical background. I am particularly interested in capturing the constant transitions the human body undergoes throughout its life. I mainly create works on paper. In 2014, I created an art hub in Paris for local and international artists interested in human form, called Life Drawing Montmartre. As part of the hub I run life drawing workshops, human anatomy for artists classes and events, including drawing marathons, art exhibitions and contests. My goal is to introduce the varied approaches to drawing the human figure to a wider public. I also offer online consultations on human anatomy and figure drawing, and accept commissions.
